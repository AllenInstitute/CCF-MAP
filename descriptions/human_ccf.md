## Human CCF

This atlas uses the [HCP **template**](https://www.humanconnectome.org/study/hcp-young-adult/document/hcp-young-adult-2025-release) from the August 2025 release, which is an averaged template from 1071 young adult brains scanned at 3T. This template is overlaid with basal ganglia **annotations** as defined by the [HOMBA](../docs/HOMBA_ontology_v1.md) **terminology**.  

| Data asset                      | S3 location                                                                                                                                            |
|---------------------------------|--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| HCP template                    | https://allen-atlas-assets.s3.us-west-2.amazonaws.com/templates/hmba-adult-human-mri-template/20250829/hmba_adult_human_hcp3t1071_template.nii.gz      |
| Annotation                      | https://allen-atlas-assets.s3.us-west-2.amazonaws.com/annotation-sets/hmba-adult-human-homba-annotation/20250829/compressed_masks_BG.nii.gz                               |
